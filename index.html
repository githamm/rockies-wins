Colorado Rockies vs National League West

In the 23 seasons since the Colorado Rockies joined the league in 1993, the team has posted seven winning seasons. Against National League West opponents from 1993-2015, the Rockies have a winning record against only the San Diego Padres. The Rockies' record against NL West teams, in order of winning percentage:

  • San Diego Padres, 195-183 (.516)
  • San Francisco Giants, 166-210 (.442)
  • Arizona Diamondbacks, 135-181 (.427)
  • Los Angeles Dodgers, 161-217 (.426)

The Los Angeles Dodgers and San Francisco Giants have been the bullies on the block, posting a cumulative winning record against all NL West opponents from 1993-2015. The San Diego Padres have a losing record against other NL West teams, but have a higher winning percentage than the Rockies.

Total team wins since 1993, in order of winning percentage:

  • Dodgers, 782-663 (.541)
  • Giants, 774-666 (.538)
  • Diamondbacks, 625-638 (.495)*
  • Padres, 681-761 (.472)
  • Rockies, 657-791 (.454)

* The Diamondbacks entered the league in 1998
